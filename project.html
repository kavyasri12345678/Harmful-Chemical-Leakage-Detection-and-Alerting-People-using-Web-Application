ABOUT


QESC refers to "quick escape" is an innovative stratergy which deals with the detection and alerting system during any type of industrial leakages which are harmful to the human survival.

Need of QESC in society:

Many industries store harmful chemicals , works using harmful gases . The leakage of such chemicals from the work place leads to death of many people.
Not only workers , the people living around the industries also gets affected due to this type of accidents.
So it is very important to alert the people surrounded the industry along with workers during any chemical leakage / gas leakage.
But a sufficient mechanism is not present to alert the people , some incidents like Bhopal gas tragedy , vizag lg polymers incident are best examples for this.
If this type of system exists during vizag LG polymers chemical leakage , it helps to save lives of many innocents.
Our aim is to alert the people around industries during this type of incidents.
Just alarms and sirens used by the industries with a range from 2-4km is not enough to alert all the neighbouring people


Solution Provided by QESC:

Our solution is to use catalytic sensor with Arduino so that both liquid and gas leak detection is easy.
Instead of alarms ,This application is used that alerts the users near the industries , factories etc when an chemical leakage / any fire accident occurs in working places like industries.
These application also sends the alert to the rescue teams , so that the rescue teams can reach the spot in time and control the damage.
This web application need to be pre registered by the people who lives in the surroundings of the Industry and needed to be alerted immediately after the harmful gas leak.
This application can also used in any work places like offices , college , to alert the rescue teams when there is any accident in work place.
